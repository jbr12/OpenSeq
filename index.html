About

Supporting Researchers is core to who we are

  • Our Beginnings

    Founded in 2022, OpenSeq focuses on helping researches to analyze NGS data, allowing them to publish in higher impact journals

  • Our Mission

    We are boosting research by delivering high quality NGS analyses. We believe omics should have no barriers. If you can't hire a full-time bioinformatician, contact us.

    OpenSeq is a dynamic company. In this moment we are focusing on providing the best service to our clients. In a not distant future, we will create a platform so that researchers can gain autonomy in their omics analysis.
